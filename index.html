Our Services

Tank Assembling and Installation: We Install Outlet, Inlet, Air lock release systems and Overflows.

Maintenance & Repairs: Fixing minor leaks in faucets or toilets and performing routine preventive maintenance on residential plumbing systems.

Pipework Assembly: Installing, bending, and joining plastic pipes(PPR, CPVC, Pressure pipes), and low-carbon steel pipe for both hot and cold water sytems.

Fixture Installation: Installation of household fixtures like sinks, commodes, and basic water heaters.

Drainage: Handling above-ground drainage tasks and clearing basic pipe blockages.

System Testing: Pressure testing of pipes and fittings to ensure there are no leaks in newly installed sections.

Outdoor water distribution: We design and install irrigation pipes and drips and water sprinklers
